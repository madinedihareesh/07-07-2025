Selectors:
 These selectors are used to provide styles for a perticuler/group of elemnts in webpage

 Types of Selectors:
A. Genral selectors:
 1.Global/universal selectors (*)
 2.Tag/Element selectors (name fo the tag)
 3.id selectors (#)
 4.class selectors (.)
 5.Attribute selectors ([])

B. Combination Selectors:
syntax:
selector:pseudo element{}
1.Psuedo Class selectors: (:)
  a.active 
  b.visited
  c.hover
  d.first-child
  e.last-child
  f.nth-chilid()
2.psuedo Elements:(::)
  a. before
  b.after
  c.first letter
  d.first line
  e.marker
  f.place holder  
3. Relationship selectors:
   a. Desendent ( )
   b.adjesent(+)
   c.direct child(>)
